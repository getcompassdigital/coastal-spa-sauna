Questions, answered

Before you make room.

How much preparation does a hot tub site need?

Most spas need a level, load-bearing surface, a clear delivery path and appropriate electrical service. Our consultation identifies those needs before delivery is scheduled.

Are delivery and setup included?

Standard local delivery, positioning, setup and a full owner orientation are included in this fictional demonstration offer. Complex access or crane placement would be quoted separately.

How do I choose between 120V and 240V?

A 120V spa can simplify installation in compact settings, while 240V generally supports faster heating and stronger simultaneous operation. We review the tradeoff for your climate and routine.

Can you coordinate with my contractor or electrician?

Yes. We can provide placement and electrical requirements and coordinate the sequence so the site is ready before delivery day.
